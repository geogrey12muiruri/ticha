## User Experience Principles

### 1. **Simplicity First**
- Clear navigation
- Minimal clicks to complete actions
- Intuitive interface

### 2. **Mobile-First**
- Works on low-end phones
- Touch-friendly
- Offline-capable

### 3. **Trust & Transparency**
- Show why matches are shown
- Clear eligibility requirements
- Verified scholarship badges

### 4. **Action-Oriented**
- Clear CTAs
- Progress indicators
- Next steps always visible

### 5. **Helpful Guidance**
- Tooltips and help text
- Step-by-step instructions
- AI assistance available
