## Application Handling: What We Actually Do

### ‚ùå **We DON'T Submit Applications**

**Why Not:**
1. **Legal Liability**: If we submit and something goes wrong, we're liable
2. **Provider Diversity**: Each has different submission methods
3. **Document Privacy**: Can't store sensitive documents
4. **Verification**: Can't verify student information

### ‚úÖ **We DO Support Applications**

**What We Provide:**
1. **Forms**: Download/view application forms
2. **Guidance**: Step-by-step instructions
3. **Templates**: Document templates
4. **Tracking**: Students mark "I applied" and track status
5. **Reminders**: Deadline notifications

**What Students Do:**
1. Find scholarship on our platform
2. Download application form
3. Fill form themselves
4. Submit to provider directly
5. Track status in our system (manual entry)
